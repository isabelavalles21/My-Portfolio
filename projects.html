Project 1: Clueless

Created for HackHive 2025, Clueless is an AI-powered platform that acts as your own personal stylist, analyzing your wardrobe, personal style and preferences to suggest the perfect outfits for any occasion. It considers factors like weather, upcoming events, and current fashion trends to suggest looks that fit the users lifestyle. Used React.js for front end, Firebase for storage, and a python with flask to make calls to out model.
